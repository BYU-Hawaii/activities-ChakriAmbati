Equipment Needed

  • Dumbbells (5-10 lbs)
  • Yoga mat
  • Water bottle

Workout Steps

  1. Warm-up: 5 minutes of jogging in place or jumping jacks
  2. Squats: 3 sets of 10 reps
  3. Push-ups: 3 sets of 8 reps
  4. Standing Dumbbell Shoulder Press: 3 sets of 10 reps
  5. Plank: Hold for 30 seconds, 3 sets
  6. Glute Bridges: 3 sets of 12 reps
  7. Cool down: 5 minutes of stretching
